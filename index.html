How FoodShare Works

Our simple three-step process connects excess food with those who need it most

1

Register & List Food

Restaurants and function halls register and list their excess food availability

2

NGOs Coordinate

Verified NGOs receive notifications and coordinate pickup and delivery

3

Deliver to Orphanages

Food is safely delivered to orphanages and community centers
