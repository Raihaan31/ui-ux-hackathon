## ğŸ“‹ Usage Guide

### For Restaurants & Food Donors

1. *Visit the Website*
   - Navigate to the homepage
   - Click "Donate Food" button

2. *Complete Donation Form*
   - Step 1: Enter restaurant details and contact information
   - Step 2: Provide food details, quantity, and safety information
   - Step 3: Schedule pickup with preferred date and urgency level
   - Step 4: Review all information and submit

3. *Receive Confirmation*
   - Get reference ID for tracking
   - Expect contact from NGO partners within 2 hours

### For NGOs & Partners

- Explore partnership opportunities through the website
- Contact through provided channels for collaboration
- Access the platform for coordinating pickups
